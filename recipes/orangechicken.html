Simple Orange Chicken

This is a simple orange chicken recipe that your family will love! This recipe will only take you about 45 minutes to create, a great meal for when you get home from work!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Wegmans Orange Chicken Sauce
  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • Oranges
  • Light Olive Oil
  • White Rice

Instructions

  1. Cook your white rice, usually takes about 20 minutes.
  2. Put 3 table spoons of olive oil in wok with medium flame.
  3. Once oil is starting to waver, plcace sliced chicken in wok and stir every minute or two.
  4. Once chicken is almost fully cooked, place onions and broccoli in wok, stir every other minute untill broccoli is soft.
  5. When chicken is cooked thouroughly, and brocolli is cooked to your liking add the Wegmans Orange Chicken Sauce. Cook for 3 minutes and stir frequently.
  6. Server over whie rice and garnish with orange slices.
  7. Eat Up!
