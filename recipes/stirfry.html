Stir Fry

My Dads Stir Fry

This is a delicious stir fry recipe that was passed down from my father to me. With these fresh prepped veggies, glorious spices, chicken, and white rice you will have an amazing dish to display at your family dinner tonight!

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • white pepper
  • ginger
  • veggies
    • onion
    • carrots
    • broccoli
    • snow peas
    • water chestnuts
    • zucchini
    • red bell pepper
  • shrimp or chicken (or both)
  • sesame oil & light olive oil
  • white rice
  • lemon

Steps

  1. Heat up the wok on medium heat, with 3 tablespoons of sesame oil. Then add the chicken base, pepper, ginger, and garlic and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  2. Put in the chicken and mix it around every few minutes until it is almost cooked. Then add all of the prepped veggies to the wok. After that add three table spoons of soy sauce and mix evenly. Let it cook for a few minutes then stir.
  3. Once stir fry is done cooking put the rue in the wok and stir it up, this will tighten the stir fry.
  4. Serve over hot rice, and wallah! You have yourself a delicious stir fry dinner!
